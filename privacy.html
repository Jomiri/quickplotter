Privacy statement

Personal information

Quickplotter.com does not handle or collect any personal information (email, name, address, etc.) of its users apart from visitor statistics (see below). Quickplotter.com is a static website with no server-side logic so it is impossible to submit any personal information even accidentally.

Visitor tracking

Quickplotter.com keeps track of visitor statistics in two ways:

  1. using server logs provided by the hosting service (AWS) and
  2. with Google Analytics.
Google Analytics uses cookies stored on your computer to monitor your use of the site. You can read how Google Analytics tracks you here: How Google used data when you use our partner's sites or apps. To opt out of tracking by Google Analytics, you can install the Google Analytics blocking add-on for your browser.

Plotting data and settings

Quickplotter.com does not upload either your numeric data, plot settings or the generated figures which all stay on your own computer.

User feedback

If you decide to send me feedback or ask a question via email (which is warmly welcome!), I promise that

  • I will never share your email address or the content of your message with third parties unless you grant me permission to do so.
  • I will use your email address only to contact you about issues relevant to your question or feedback.
I may ask you to share your question to create a Frequently Asked Questions section for this site.
